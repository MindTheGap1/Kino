FAQ

What is Kino?

Kino is a movie rental website where you can rent the newest movies for a limited amount of time of your choice! No need for a subscription!


When does my rental period begin?

The rental period of purchased movies begins as soon as you press the play button. You can check how long is left of your rental in the "My Collection" page.


What happens when my rental period ends?

Once the rental period is over, you will no longer have access to the movie that you have rented unless you rent it again.


Can I extend my rental period?

We cannot extend your period currently but your progress is saved even if you are not currently renting the movie so you can rent it again.


Can I purchase a movie to keep?

Unfortunately we do not sell the movies, only limited time rentals.


If I close my tab, will my progress into the movie be lost?

No! We keep track of where you are in each movie so you can jump back right where you left off, anytime and anywhere!


Can I see which movies I have previously rented?

Yes, in the "My Account" page you can find information regarding previous and current orders.


Can anyone else see my ratings?

No other users will be able to see what you have rated personally but your rating will be used to update the average rating of the movie.
